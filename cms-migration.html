SEO-Safe CMS Migration & Rebrand

Developer & Consultant · Blue Coding / Resolve Digital · 2020–2021

WordPress (ACF) 301 Redirect Mapping Lead Gen SEO Landing Pages

The problem

The team needed to move off October CMS and rebrand at the same time. Traffic and inbound leads depended on organic visibility. A bad migration could kill both.

What I did

  • Audited URLs, content, and backlinks to understand what could not break.
  • Moved the site into WordPress with structured, reusable ACF blocks instead of hard-coded pages.
  • Rebuilt high-intent landing pages and forms with clearer messaging and faster load.
  • Implemented redirect mapping (301s) to keep authority and avoid SEO loss at launch.
  • Spun up a static marketing sub-site in Jekyll / CloudCannon for a luxury client, so they could publish without dev help.

Why it mattered

Marketing got control. They could add and tweak landing pages without opening tickets. Sales got cleaner inbound. And the domain didn’t tank in search during the move.

Result

Smooth relaunch. No major ranking loss. Faster handoff from marketing concept to live, tested page.
