SEO Audit, Landing Flow & Inbound Funnel

Webmaster & SEO · Predictable Revenue · 2018–2020

On-Page SEO Performance Fixes Elementor Chatbot Funnel Conversion Flow

The problem

Traffic existed, but conversion was weak. Pages didn’t match what people were actually searching for. Lead capture felt generic and slow.

What I did

  • Ran a technical SEO audit: crawl issues, slow templates, weak internal linking.
  • Rebuilt high-intent landing pages in Elementor to align with specific services and pain points.
  • Added chatbot logic to qualify visitors and route them to sales in real time instead of waiting on a form follow-up.
  • Set up tracking so we could see which topics actually drove pipeline, not just clicks.

Why it mattered

This wasn’t just “more traffic.” It was better traffic with a clearer path into a live conversation.

Result

Cleaner SEO footprint, faster load, and a direct chat path for high-intent visitors.
